# Product Principles

AI Native Platform Research Lab is an enterprise research product, not a generic CRUD board.

Its purpose is to help a team accumulate research evidence, opinions, decisions, and frameworks while studying how planning work changes in an AI Native operating model.

## Product Direction

- The product must support team research and decision accumulation.
- The product must make the current research state easy to scan.
- The product must distinguish temporary opinions from approved decisions.
- The product must help the team move from research topics to roadmap items and reusable frameworks.
- The product must stay vendor-neutral and not depend on a specific AI tool as a product assumption.

## Collaboration Principles

- Korean is the default collaboration language.
- English technical terms may be kept when they are clearer than translated terms.
- Small decisions should be recorded continuously.
- Personal opinions and team decisions must remain visibly separate.
- Approved frameworks should be based on accumulated research, opinions, and decisions.

## Domain Concepts

- Topic: a research subject the team is investigating.
- Roadmap: a staged work plan connected to a topic.
- Opinion: an individual or team member view that is not yet an approved decision.
- Decision: an approved record of team alignment.
- Framework: a structured output derived from research and decisions.
- Member: a user or participant with a role and status.

## Product Boundary

The product philosophy does not define API behavior, Spreadsheet schema, frontend implementation, Git workflow, or Codex execution steps. Those rules belong to the `.ai/` documentation set.
